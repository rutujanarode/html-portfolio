The Best Movies according to Rutuja

My top 3 movies of recent times.

Neerja

Real story on the incident of hijacked plane which portrays the bravery of a air hostess "Neerja" who saved many lives by sacrificing her live.

Andhadhun

Great story. Superb acting by all the actors of the film specially Ayushman Khurana and Tabu.

Srikanth

Real life story of a blind man "Srikanth Bola" who was rejected by society. But he proved himself. Inspirational story.. Must watch movie.
